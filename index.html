Welcome to Ashok Travel

Maharashtra, the land of the Marathas, is a treasure trove of natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. From the majestic Western Ghats to the sun-kissed beaches
of the Konkan coast, Maharashtra has a plethora of tourist attractions that cater to all kinds of travelers.
Visit the iconic Gateway of India in Mumbai, explore the ancient caves of Ajanta and Ellora, or trek to the scenic hill stations of Lonavala and Khandala. Maharashtra is also home to several pilgrimage sites, including the revered Shirdi Sai Baba temple and the sacred city of Nashik. Whether you're a history buff, a nature lover, or a thrill-seeker, Maharashtra has something for everyone.
